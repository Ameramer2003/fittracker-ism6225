What We Offer

Built for Athletes.
Powered by Data.

📈

Progress Analytics

Interactive charts powered by Chart.js show your real workout trends pulled directly from your logged data. Weekly calories, duration trends, and category mix all update as you train.

📚

Exercise Library

Browse hundreds of exercises fetched live from the wger REST API. Filter by muscle group, view muscles targeted and equipment needed, then add any exercise directly to your workout log.

🤖

AI Coach

FitBot is your Botpress-powered AI assistant trained on a curated exercise dataset. Ask about routines, form, muscles targeted, and equipment requirements.

🗂️

Full CRUD Workout Log

Create, view, edit, and delete workout entries with data persisted to localStorage. Your workout history survives browser refreshes and is used to power the analytics charts.

📱

Fully Responsive

FitTracker works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile — track workouts anywhere, anytime with a clean dark athletic interface.

💾

Smart Filtering

Search and filter your workout log by exercise name, category, or notes. The Exercise Library also filters by muscle group in real-time from the live API.
