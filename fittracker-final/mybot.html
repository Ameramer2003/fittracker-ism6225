About FitBot

FitBot is built on Botpress and trained on a 10-row curated exercise dataset covering exercise names, categories, difficulty levels, equipment, muscle groups, sets, reps, and estimated calories per session. It is designed to answer common fitness questions and help users plan their workout sessions.
